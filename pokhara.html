Pokhara

Phewa Lake

The lake’s shores are lined with hotels, restaurants, and shops, catering to both domestic and international visitors. Popular activities include boating, fishing, and paragliding, making it a hub for adventure and relaxation. Its serene ambiance and cultural significance, including the Tal Barahi Temple located on an island in the lake, further enhance its appeal.

Sarangkot

Nepal, renowned for its stunning views of the Annapurna mountain range and Machhapuchhre. It is a favored spot for witnessing breathtaking sunrises, with visitors often praising the panoramic vistas and the serene atmosphere. The site is easily accessible via a free shuttle bus from Pokhara, making it a convenient destination for tourists

Davis Falls

The Davis Falls Located approximately 2 kilometers north from the lakeside, this waterfall is renowned for its unique feature of forming a 500-foot-long underground tunnel as it reaches its base. Davis Falls is a must-visit site for nature lovers, attracting thousands of tourists annually.
